Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wageify?

Wageify is an easy-to-use payroll software that provides customizable Excel templates for managing payroll. It simplifies the process of calculating wages, taxes, and deductions, saving you time and reducing errors. It’s perfect for accountants looking for a reliable and straightforward payroll solution.

Is Wageify suitable for businesses of all sizes?

Yes! Wageify's Excel templates are flexible and can be tailored to suit businesses of any size. Whether you're handling payroll for a small startup or a large corporation, our templates help streamline payroll processes and ensure accuracy, no matter the complexity.

How does Wageify help accountants?

Wageify helps accountants save time by automating complex payroll tasks. Our Excel templates are pre-set to calculate taxes, deductions, and net pay, reducing the chance for human error. This means you can process payroll faster and more efficiently, without needing to build custom spreadsheets from scratch.

Is it difficult to use Wageify?

No, it's designed for simplicity. Even if you're new to payroll, our templates and support resources make it easy to get started.

Can I use Wageify for businesses anywhere?

Currently, Wageify is only available for businesses based in Ontario (Canada), as our templates are tailored to meet Ontario’s payroll laws and tax regulations. However, we are actively working on expanding our offerings, and in the near future, we plan to make Wageify available across Canada and the United States.

Are there any ongoing costs with Wageify?

No, there are no ongoing subscription fees with Wageify. You simply purchase our Excel templates, and you can use them as long as you need without worrying about additional charges. This makes it a cost-effective payroll solution for accountants and businesses.
